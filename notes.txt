TYPES OF BINDING 
    - One Way Binding
        {{}} --> Known as interpolation binding (one way binding) (component to view)
        []  -->  Property Binding (On way binding) (component to view) (Can manipulate attributes of in DOM) 
        ()  --> Event binding (On way binding) (view to component)
    -Two Way Binding
        [()] --> Two way binding (component to view and vise versa)
